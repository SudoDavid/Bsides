Our Inaugural Event!

Thank you for your interest in sponsoring the first Security BSides event in Fort Myers, FL

BSides Fort Myers provides a regional forum for all information security enthusiasts to connect with other like-minded individuals while gaining up-to-date info within the security domain. Additionally, this event enables sponsors to engage comfortably and efficiently with our fast-growing SWFL security community.

Download Sponsor Application With Submission Instructions



The nature of our smaller, community-focused event provides our sponsors with the opportunity to be a big fish in a small but very active pond. We’ve removed the high barriers to entry for our participants, and we’ve done the same for our sponsors.

  • Exposure to the Southwest Florida and Global Security Community
  • Your Name and Logo “Up in Lights”
  • The Feel-Good Factor
  • Unique Sponsorship Levels Fit Made For You

Sponsor Levels Offerings Explained:

  • Logo on Sponsorship Page

    Under bsidesftmyers.com/sponsors, you logo will be displayed and linked to your company URL.

  • Logo In-Between Session Slideshow

    During the Conference, between presentations, a slides show featuring your logo will be displayed.

  • Logo on Badge

    Every security conference needs official badges to indicate who’s registered, we want to put your logo on the back to remind them who helped bring BSides to Fort Myers.

  • Logo on Shirts

    Official conference shirts with gold and platinum sponsors printed on the back

  • Table and Banner Placement

    Setup a table to showoff your business, products, solutions, recruitment needs, or other. Platinum sponsors will have their logos on one or more banners at the entrance and registration desk. Gold and Silver sponsors will have their logos on one or more banners located around the conference.

  • Social Media Shoutouts

    As a sponsor, we want to let everyone know on our social media accounts who helped bring BSides to Fort Myers.

  • Event & Workshop Tickets

    During BSides, enjoy tickets to explore our workshops or the presentations at the conference.

  • Small Room Reservation

    An informal setting away from vendor row for sponsors looking to meet potential B2B, new hires, or just discuss the state of Cybersecurity in 15 minute loops.

  • Expo Passes

    Bring a coworker, friend, or a loved one to our family fun event to visit the expo booths, network, or participate in events!
