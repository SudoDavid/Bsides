About B-Sides Fort Myers

BSides Fort Myers 2022 is the premier information security event on the Paradise Coast of Southwest Florida. This initiative was triggered by the need of such an event in a region experiencing explosive growth both in population and businesses, from a healthy start-up ecosystem to global corporations. BSides Fort Myers provides a diverse and inclusive information security conference welcoming anyone and everyone that is interested in security. This is a platform for first-time speakers, students, and new and experienced professionals to present their work in a friendly and welcoming environment. We welcome all participants.

WHAT IS SECURITY B-SIDES?

Each BSides is a community-driven framework for building events for and by information security community members. The goal is to expand the spectrum of conversation beyond the traditional confines of space and time. It creates opportunities for individuals to both present and participate in an intimate atmosphere that encourages collaboration. It is an intense event with discussions, demos, and interaction from participants. It is where conversations for the next-big-thing are happening.

The conference will provide approximately 150 to 300 participants with a speaking track that will cover a wide range of information security disciplines and topics, with multiple workshops, a CTF competition, and much more! Since our goal is to keep the event as low-cost as possible to participants, we are asking sponsors to generously support our event.

History of Security B-Sides

Security BSides was born out of a desire to remove barriers to entry and create a community of active participants. We believe that making it easier for everyone to participate in the security community enables great things. What started as a small gathering of folks at one event in 2009 has grown to more than 275 BSides events in 84 cities, in 19 countries in just eight years, and the number of events continues to grow as participants realise they can create a community anywhere, be it in Augusta or Zurich.

While every BSides event is unique and independently run, each is committed to being a community- organized, volunteer-driven, and corporately funded forum for expanding the spectrum of information security conversations.

Call for Presenters

The purpose of the BSides Fort Myers Conference is for bridging the knowledge gap between academia and industry expertise, promoting cybersecurity as it relates to the greater information technology field. What better way than inviting consultants, hackers, hobbyists, academics, students, and public-sector type folks to a conference hall to share their ideas, experiences and knowledge?

We want BSides Fort Myers 2022 to be an inclusive and diverse conference, both in terms of its participants and content. We are interested in talks covering all aspects of security. When you submit, you can decide on your talk length. We are happy to support anything from a 10-minute lightning talk through to a 60-minute demonstration or talk. In addition to this, we are also interested in submissions for 2-hour, 4-hour, and 8-hour workshops.
