- Call For Papers! -

The purpose of the BSides Fort Myers Conference is for bridging the knowledge gap between academia and industry expertise, promoting cybersecurity as it relates to the greater information technology field. What better way than inviting consultants, hackers, hobbyists, academics, students, and public-sector type folks to a conference hall to share their ideas, experiences and knowledge?

Submit Now

Diversity and Inclusivity:

We are committed to promoting diversity and inclusivity in the security community. We are particularly keen to receive submissions from women, LGBTQI, people of color, younger, older, financially less resourced, and other groups who are underrepresented in the security and wider tech industry. We believe that information security outcomes are significantly improved when we are inclusive and listen to the ideas, experiences and knowledge from as diverse group of people as possible. All submissions will be reviewed by our CFP review board.

CFP:Topics, Format, and Length

We want BSides Fort Myers 2022 to be an inclusive and diverse conference, both in terms of its participants and content. We are interested in talks covering all aspects of security. When you submit, you can decide on your talk length. We are happy to support anything from a 10-minute lightning talk through to a 60-minute demonstration or talk. In addition to this, we are also interested in submissions for 2-hour, 4-hour, and 8-hour workshops. Here are a few ideas of talks or workshops just to get the brain juice flowing Keep in mind these are just examples – not a complete list. We are interested in all talks related to security:


  • 01 Accounting, Economics, and Finance
  • 02 A.I., Machine Learning, & Decision Making
  • 03 Blockchain and Big Data
  • 04 Critical Infrastructure
  • 05 Emerging Technologies and Innovation
  • 06 Education, Curriculum, and Online Learning
  • 07 Healthcare and medical
 
  • 08 Information technology and security
  • 09 Legal, Ethical, and Social Issues
  • 10 Military science
  • 11 Operations Management
  • 12 Social behavior and organizational culture
  • 13 Supply chain management and logistics
  • 14 Resources and sustainability

Tips

Following these tips will help us understand why we should select your presentation for BSides Fort Myers 2022.
  • Give your proposal a simple and straightforward title.
  • Ensure that your talk is authentic, your peers will want original ideas, real-world scenarios, relevant examples or to learn something cool./li>
  • Include as much detail about the presentation as possible.
  • Explain why people will want to attend your talk and what they’ll take away from it.
  • Keep your proposal free of marketing, sales, buzzwords, jargon and Fear Uncertainty and Doubt (FUD).
  • Limit the scope of your talk and ensure that you cover the interesting idea, concept, technique or finding in the presentation time you have selected.
  • If you only have enough material to present for 30 minutes, please don’t propose a 60-minute talk and then stretch your material to fill time.

We are not accepting any vendor pitches or corporate cheerleading talks. You are welcome to talk briefly about what your company does or use corporate slides.

However, your talk must focus on sharing your ideas, experience and knowledge with the community, and not selling products and services.

If you made it this far through reading, go ahead and submit!
